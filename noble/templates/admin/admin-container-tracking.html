Various Statuses and Meaning
  1. Pending:
    The shipment is created but hasn't been picked up or started moving yet.
  2. Collected:
    The cargo has been collected from the sender.
  3. In Transit:
    The cargo is currently in route to its destination.
  4. Held at Customs:
    The cargo is being held by customs authorities for inspection or clearance.
  5. Delivered:
    The cargo has been successfully delivered to the recipient.
  6. Awaiting Pickup:
    The cargo is at a pickup point, waiting for the recipient to collect it.
  7. Delayed:
    The cargo has encountered an unexpected delay, such as bad weather or logistical issues.
  8. Returned to Sender:
    The cargo is being returned to the sender due to delivery failure or other reasons.
