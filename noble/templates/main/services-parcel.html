Parcel Tracking

Parcel tracking is a service designed for smaller packages that are typically transported by courier services. This service is popular for both personal and business purposes, such as online shopping deliveries or sending documents.

How It Works:

  • Shipment: The process starts when a parcel is picked up by a courier service. Each parcel is given a unique tracking number.
  • Tracking Number: The tracking number is used to monitor the parcel’s journey, from pickup through transit and delivery.
  • Tracking Updates: The tracking website provides updates at each stage of the parcel’s journey, including pickup, sorting, transit, out for delivery, and delivery confirmation.
  • Customer Notifications: Customers can opt to receive notifications via email or SMS, informing them of the parcel’s status and any potential delays.
